Classes Built For You
At Bilancia Fitness we believe that adding fitness into your life is all about balance. In different seasons of our lives, our fitness routine looks different. We are here to help you find what we are all looking for - balance!
  • We offer a great variety of fitness classes designed to keep you moving, sweating, happy, and strong.
  • We offer classes rooted in pilates, functional strength training, barre, high-intensity interval training, dance, and yoga.
  • We offer something for everyone and we can't wait to move and sweat with you.
Cardio Dance
Enjoy a dance party that combines low & high-intensity intervals and dance/fitness combinations to your favorite music! A total body workout that offers cardio endurance, muscle conditioning, balance, & flexibility! This class will be sure to make you sweat and smile.
Strength
This resistance-based circuit training class will leave you feeling accomplished and strong. You will use dumbbells, resistance bands, and your body throughout class. You'll move at a pace that will allow you to challenge yourself with heavier resistance and strengthen your body and mind.
Worke
Plan to will use dumbbells between 5lbs-15lbs, resistance bands, and your body weight combined with choreographed repetition movements for a full body workout. This class will fly by and you'll be motivated to complete one more rep to the beat of the music. All fitness levels are welcome!
Fusion
Signature Poise class that adds a fusion of Barreworke designed to leave you feeling strong and aligned. You will spend part of you time on the mat focused on core, legs, and upper body and the other half incorporated barre exercises and standing lower body exercises at the barre.
