Get To Know Us
Bilancia Fitness is designed to help you get a little more balance in your life. We offer a variety of fitness and yoga classes to help you move in a way that fits your life at that time. During different seasons of life our workouts may look and feel a bit different. We are all looking for some balance and we hope that Bilancia Fitness can be a little part of what you do to find yours.
Bilancia Fitness was established in 2023. Diana Dellogono acquired Studio Poise in January 2023 after teaching classes at Studio Poise for over 10 years. Big thank you to Danielle Bradley, founder of Studio Poise, for creating the studio and community she did which helped create Bilancia Fitness.
